Hi, I'm Kuromi

Front-end Developer - Designer

Hello!

My name is Isadora Vieira and I'm from the countryside of São Paulo, Brazil. Since I was young, I've always been passionate about art and technology. Over the years, I've explored different creative areas — such as drawing, comics, video editing, and content production for the internet. I started my studies in front-end development at the age of 17, but I had to temporarily interrupt my journey due to technical limitations. Even with interest and dedication, the resources I had at the time were not enough to continue, which led me to focus my attention on other paths for a while. I'm currently unemployed and continue to study front-end on my own, in a flexible routine throughout the day. I dedicate a few hours to studying whenever possible, trying to maintain a healthy consistency. I use tools like YouTube and ChatGPT to deepen my knowledge, developing personal projects and gradually evolving in the programming area. My goal for the future is to become a professional full stack developer, expanding my skills in both front-end and back-end. I also have a great interest in game development, an area that has always fascinated me and in which I intend to specialize over time. I use the nickname Kuromi, inspired by the character from the Hello Kitty universe. Feel free to follow my projects here on GitHub. Thank you for being part of my journey!
